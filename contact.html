FAQ

How can I contact customer support?
You can reach our customer support team by emailing on Felinefindsupport@gmail.com or by calling our toll-free number 1-800-123-4567. We are available to assist you from Monday to Friday, 9 AM to 5 PM and we aim to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours.
Are your products safe for cats with allergies or sensitivities?
We offer a variety of products suitable for cats with allergies or sensitivities. However, we recommend reviewing the product descriptions and ingredient lists carefully to ensure they meet your cat's specific dietary requirements. If you have any concerns, consult with your veterinarian before making a purchase of a cat item.
Can I purchase products directly from the website?
No, the Feline Finds website serves as a platform to showcase various products for cats. To make a purchase, please contact the seller directly using the WhatsApp number provided. They will guide you through the purchasing process and arrange the transaction with you.
What is the response time for WhatsApp inquiries?
The response time may vary depending on the seller's availability and workload. However, sellers strive to respond to inquiries as soon as possible. If you don't receive an immediate response, please allow some time for the seller to get back to you. Rest assured, they will reply to your inquiry.
How frequently are new products added to the website?
New products are added regularly as sellers update their inventory. Feline Finds strives to keep the product selection fresh and exciting for users. It's recommended to check back frequently or follow the website's social media accounts for updates on newly added products and any special offers.
